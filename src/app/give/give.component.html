Why is giving important?

As a Church, we have a platform set up to foster giving among members and non-members of the Church. This system is termed "Partnership". Partnership is sharing the burden of a vision to see and bring it to its accomplishment, making it easy. This is done in sacrifices of time, resources and labour! Hence, in line with the vision and mission at HeraldsNation of heralding the life of God and raising the total man in Christ, these will only be accomplished by the sacrifices of men via their resources in ensuring that the word of this truth is sent forth from us to the different parts of the world. There were deliberate, systematic and consistent givers to the ministry of Jesus and Apostle Paul in their days. People partnered with Paul and gave to his ministry on a regular basis. (See Phil. 4:19 & 1Cor 16:2). One of the reasons God blesses you is so that you can, with your resources, further His kingdom on earth. It is therefore a proper thing to give and commit your resources to God's work. In this regard, the church has two systems of partnership viz. Venue Partnership and General Partnership.
